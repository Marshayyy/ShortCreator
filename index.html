Shorts Builder
Script → animated space scenes → captions → voiceover → downloadable video.

Script

01
The script auto-splits into scenes by sentence. Aim for 5-9 sentences for a 30-45 second short.

Scene Timeline

02
Each scene defaults to a procedural space backdrop with a slow zoom animation. Tap Generate AI Images for All Scenes to create a matching AI image per scene (powered by Puter.js — free, may ask you to sign in with a free Puter account on first use), or tap Upload Image on any scene to use your own photo. Re-run "Build Scenes" anytime after editing the script above (note: this resets images).

Voiceover

03
Uses your device's built-in voices — free, no key, no account. Quality varies by phone/browser, but it's instant.

Render

04
Ready to render.
Renders as WebM video. Browser text-to-speech can't be captured into the file directly — see the note after rendering for how to add the voice track.
